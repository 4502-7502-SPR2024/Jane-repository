My Favorite Sitcoms

I love sitcoms. They're great to toss on in the background while doing chores or to watch with friends and family. Here is a list of some of my favorites and where you can stream them!

The Office

(2005-2013) Starring: Steve Carell, Rainn Wilson, John Krasinski and Jenna Fischer
Stream: Peacock

Modern Family

(2009-2020) Starring: Ty Burrell, Julie Bowen, Ed O'Neill, Jesse Tyler Ferguson and Sofia Vergara
Stream: Hulu

Parks and Recreation

(2009-2015) Starring: Amy Poehler, Nick Offerman, Aubrey Plaza, Adam Scott and Chris Pratt
Stream: Peacock

Abbott Elementary

(2021-) Starring: Quinta Brunson, Tyler James Williams, Lisa Ann Walter and Janelle James
Steam: Hulu

It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia

(2005-) Starring: Danny Devito, Charlie Day, Glenn Howerton, Rob McElhenney and Kaitlin Olson
Stream: Hulu

Arrested Development

(2003-2006) Starring: Jason Bateman, Michael Cera, Will Arnett and Jessica Walter
Stream: Netflix

Brooklyn 99

(2013-2021) Starring: Andre Braugher, Andy Samberg, Melissa Fumero and Terry Crews
Stream: Peacock
