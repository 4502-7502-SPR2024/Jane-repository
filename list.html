My Favorite Sitcoms

Skip to content
I love sitcoms. They're great to toss on in the background while doing chores or to watch with friends and family. Here is a list of some of my favorites and where you can stream them!

The Office

(2005-2013) Starring: Steve Carell, Rainn Wilson, John Krasinski and Jenna Fischer
A documentary-style comedy about disgruntled office employees and their ridiculous boss.
Stream: Peacock

Modern Family

(2009-2020) Starring: Ty Burrell, Julie Bowen, Ed O'Neill, Jesse Tyler Ferguson and Sofia Vergara
The story of a diverse extended family as they argue, laugh and support one another.
Stream: Hulu

Parks and Recreation

(2009-2015) Starring: Amy Poehler, Nick Offerman, Aubrey Plaza, Adam Scott and Chris Pratt
A team of local government employees in the small town of Pawnee, Indiana, navigating challenges, romance and the ridiculous requests of the town.
Stream: Peacock

Abbott Elementary

(2021-) Starring: Quinta Brunson, Tyler James Williams, Lisa Ann Walter and Janelle James
A mockumentary style comedy about teachers at an underfunded elementary school.
Steam: Hulu

It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia

(2005-) Starring: Danny Devito, Charlie Day, Glenn Howerton, Rob McElhenney and Kaitlin Olson
A raunchy comedy about "the gang", 5 horrible people running a bar in Philly.
Stream: Hulu

Arrested Development

(2003-2006) Starring: Jason Bateman, Michael Cera, Will Arnett and Jessica Walter
After the patriarch of the family company is arrested for embezzlement, a son must take over his position in the company and within the family.
Stream: Netflix

Brooklyn 99

(2013-2021) Starring: Andre Braugher, Andy Samberg, Melissa Fumero and Terry Crews
The story of talented detectives in a New York precinct
Stream: Peacock
